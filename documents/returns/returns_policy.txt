# Returns and Exchanges Policy v3.0

## Section 1: Return Window
Standard items may be returned within 30 days of delivery.
Electronics and software licenses must be returned within 14 days, unopened.
Perishable goods, digital downloads, and personalized items are non-returnable.

## Section 2: Return Eligibility
Items must be in original, unused condition with all original packaging and tags.
Damaged items due to customer misuse are not eligible for return.
Items marked as "Final Sale" are non-returnable and non-exchangeable.

## Section 3: Return Process
1. Log in to your account and navigate to Order History.
2. Select the order and click "Initiate Return".
3. Choose the return reason and preferred resolution (refund or exchange).
4. Print the prepaid return label (eligible orders only).
5. Drop off at any authorized carrier location within 7 days.

## Section 4: Refund Timelines
Once the returned item is received and inspected (3-5 business days):
- Credit card refunds: 5-7 business days
- Store credit: issued immediately upon inspection approval

## Section 5: Exchanges
Exchanges are subject to stock availability.
Price differences are charged or refunded accordingly.
Refer to Section 4 for refund timelines on exchange price differences.

## Section 6: Defective Items
Defective items may be returned at any time within the warranty period (12 months).
Contact returns@company.com with photos of the defect for expedited processing.
See Section 3 for the standard return process; defective items skip the eligibility check.
