# Billing Policy v2.1

## Section 1: Payment Terms
All invoices are due within 30 days of the billing date. Late payments incur a 1.5% monthly fee.
Accepted payment methods: credit card, debit card, bank transfer, PayPal.

## Section 2: Subscription Billing
Subscriptions are billed on the same date each month (or annually for yearly plans).
Customers will receive an invoice email 7 days before the billing date.
If payment fails, a retry is attempted after 3 days. After 3 failed attempts, the account is suspended.

## Section 3: Refund Policy
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.
Full refunds are available within 14 days of purchase for unused services.
Partial refunds are calculated pro-rata for early cancellation of annual plans.

## Section 4: Dispute Resolution
Billing disputes must be raised within 60 days of the invoice date.
Submit disputes via email to billing@company.com or through the account portal.
Disputed amounts are placed on hold during investigation (up to 30 business days).

## Section 5: Duplicate Charges
If you see a duplicate charge, contact billing support immediately with your invoice number.
Duplicate charges are refunded within 2 business days upon verification.
See Section 3 for refund timelines.

## Section 6: Tax and VAT
Applicable taxes are calculated based on the customer's billing address.
Tax-exempt customers must submit their exemption certificate to billing@company.com.
