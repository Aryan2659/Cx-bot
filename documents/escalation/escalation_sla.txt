# Escalation and SLA Policy v1.4

## Section 1: Escalation Triggers
An issue is escalated when:
- The customer requests to speak with a manager or senior agent.
- An issue remains unresolved after 2 contact attempts.
- The transaction value exceeds $500.
- The customer reports legal, compliance, or safety concerns.

## Section 2: Response SLAs
Priority 1 (Critical - safety, legal, fraud): Response within 2 hours, resolution within 24 hours.
Priority 2 (High - unresolved billing/returns over $200): Response within 4 hours, resolution within 48 hours.
Priority 3 (Standard - general unresolved issues): Response within 1 business day, resolution within 5 business days.

## Section 3: Escalation Process
1. Escalation agent creates a ticket with full conversation history.
2. Ticket is assigned to a senior support specialist.
3. Customer is notified via email with ticket ID and expected response time.
4. Senior specialist contacts customer via phone or email per SLA.

## Section 4: Compensation Guidelines
Customers experiencing service failures may be eligible for:
- Service credit (up to 1 month free for subscription customers).
- Expedited shipping on next order.
- Partial refund at manager discretion (up to 25% of order value).
All compensation requires manager approval. See Section 3 for escalation process.

## Section 5: Regulatory Complaints
For complaints involving data privacy (GDPR/CCPA), contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@company.com.
Financial service complaints must be filed per applicable regulatory guidelines.
Escalate all regulatory complaints immediately to Priority 1 per Section 2.
